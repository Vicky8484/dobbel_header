About HSKT

Well, we're just a thrift store. Not much else to it really. Wait, actually! We're cheaper than most thrift stores out here. I mean, thats what it was always about, right?


Well... not really. The essence of thrifting is finding hidden gems that were once loved by someone else. Someone had an amazing first date wearing that dress. Someone won a dance competition wearing those shoes. Someone ripped those jeans seven years ago to make them look cooler. And now they're yours.


That's what its really about. (But in this economy.. very low prices is a huge bonus).
